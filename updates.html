HOPE Details

HOPE

Last updated July 9th, 2026

Content warning: Do not read if sensitive to dark topics.

Hi all. Recently, I have been busy with a new project, HOPE. It's been quite a while into development, and it started back in December 2024. However, I began development in October 2025, and recently I've been a little bit of a recluse in game development. I'm not completely off the face of this Earth, haha.

I have been collaborating with glasseshead on a new project named HOPE.

HOPE is a JRPG that focuses more on what the story can do to the player, and the impacts of a ludonarrative on us as individuals. HOPE is mainly inspired by UNDERTALE, which I grew up with. I quite admire the works of Toby Fox, including DELTARUNE. However, I also took inspiration from OMORI by OMOCAT, which inspired me the most to start this project. I was also inspired by Doki Doki Literature Club and That Dragon, Cancer.

HOPE focuses on the mental struggles of Dorian, who suffers from his SOUL, depression, and suicidal ideation. Dorian's life isn't all depressing. He does have a love interest, and he tries to cope with his social anxiety and his depression whenever he tries to talk to them.

There's no official release date, and I'm not even sure if this will ever be finished, but if it does, that would mean a lot to me.

WarriorBub Discontinuation

WarriorBub

Last updated June 13th, 2026

Unfortunately, I have decided to discontinue WarriorBub, my previous Discord bot. This was after continued deterioration of progress, lack of support, and lack of time to work on the project more and more. As I move to my more recent, important project, I decided it was best to close down WarriorBub for good. I never did get to finish WarriorBub, and I probably will never, but I want to thank everyone who has been with WarriorBub all the way and supported its early development.

Once again, it has been a great time with WarriorBub, and I look forward to what may come in the future.
